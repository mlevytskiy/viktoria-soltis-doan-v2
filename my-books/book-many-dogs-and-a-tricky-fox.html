Many Dogs and a Tricky Fox

Big Wimmelbook let kids ages 2 to 10 be the storytellers with hours of seek-and-find hands-on learning—and fun! This is a wonderful, funny story in pictures about the life of the Dogs.

Wimmelbook, otherwise known as the shimmering book, ideal for getting acquainted with the surrounding world, development of speech and imagination. Examining this book-picture enriched with humor and details is a pleasure. This funny story about dogs' lives contains a lot of hidden scenes and exercises. Find a tricky fox in every scene, and also find lost items and dog’s “cupcakes”.

This is a wonderful book for family reading and laughing.

Packed with things to spot and funny story about dogs! The books are perfect for boys, girls, early readers, primary school students, and even toddlers.
