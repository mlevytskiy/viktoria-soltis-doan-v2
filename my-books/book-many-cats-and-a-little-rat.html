Many Cats and a Little Rat

Big Wimmelbook let kids ages 2 to 10 be the storytellers with hours of seek-and-find hands-on learning—and fun!

This is a wonderful, funny story in pictures about the life of the Cats. Every resident has his own character and habits. They live in the Cathouse and are busy with their Cat's business. They are so concerned that they don't even notice that a little Rat has got into their house.

Help the Cats to find the little brawler! ★

And yet a hen, Dora, settled in the house, and she already got used to her fluffy neighbors.

Packed with things to spot and funny story about cats!

The books are perfect for boys, girls, early readers, primary school students, and even toddlers.

What Readers Says

lisa jacovsky: Very cute and interactive book! It's a bit busy but that is part of the charm. Each page encourages the reader to find items. It helps the reader learn some fun facts about cats. I enjoyed this book and loved the surprise of the interaction!

Donna Glass: What a fun book. Kids will love seeking out the hidden items. The illustrations will make them laugh, particularly the popping and farting cats. With so much activity on the pages, you'll have to read it multiple times to catch all the happenings. If you download it to your kindle fire, you'll want to turn your device sideways to get optimal viewing of the pages.

Beena Dhyani: Such a funny book giving us a peek into the “Secret life of cats”! Kids will love this book for sure! It’s beautifully illustrated with creative & hilarious attributes of cats! The most amazing thing about the book is that it is so detailed and well-thought- of! It was so much fun to read to my son! He kept laughing the whole time! Highly recommended! Kudos to the author!
