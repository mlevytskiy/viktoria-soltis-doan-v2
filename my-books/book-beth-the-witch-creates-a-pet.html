Beth the Witch Creates a Pet

Experience the magic of this sweet and funny book for little ones!

Very often we don’t notice who is beside us. After all, to take care of someone, you do not need to invent anything extraordinary. There are many animals that need our care and love around us. In return, they will become our real friends!

This funny and fascinating story teaches us to be merciful, considerate and happy. Happiness is always nearby.

Giggle and learn valuable life lessons with Beth.

Fun story and joyful illustrations make these books perfect for parents and toddlers to read together!
