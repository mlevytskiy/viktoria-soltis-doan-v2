The Dragon and The Drandma

Cute, relatable characters and an irresistibly cool dragon. What more could a kid ask for?

In "The Dragon and the Grandma" follow the heartwarming journey of Grandma Astrid as she discovers the true meaning of love, care, and acceptance in this enchanting tale.

With whimsical and funny moments, "The Dragon and the Grandma" will delight young readers and captivate their imagination. Astrid's adventures with her peculiar pet challenge her at first, but through a series of events, she discovers the power of pure love and compassion. As their bond deepens, Astrid learns to embrace the Dragon's unique qualities, realizing that appearances do not define the worth of a friendship.

What Readers Says

Bookish: A heartwarming story about Grandma Astrid and her pet Dragon. The Dragon is quite challenging at first but read on and you will find how their unique friendship develops. Great lessons, so great book. The illustrations are amazing throughout the book!

Sheila: This book is a great way to introduce the joys and responsibilities of pet ownership to young people. I went over this book with my daughter before we got her kitten so that she would have a better sense of what to expect. I would definitely recommend any parent thinking about getting a pet for their child!

Michael B.: A funny and dynamic story about a grandmother and her pet Dragon. This is a great way to teach children that pets are not toys. They cannot be returned to the store. Pets need and deserve real love and care!
